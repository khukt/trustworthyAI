# Trustworthy AI Showcases

A showcase hub for interactive demos focused on **Trustworthy AI** in wireless/6G and smart industry contexts.

## What is Trustworthy AI?

Trustworthy AI is about building AI systems that people can rely on in real-world and mission-critical settings. In this showcase, the focus is on:

- **Robustness**: stable behavior under uncertainty, noise, or distribution shift.
- **Transparency**: understandable outputs and explainable decision behavior.
- **Security**: resilience against manipulation and adversarial misuse.
- **Reliability**: dependable performance in practical deployment scenarios.

The hub presents these principles through live, stakeholder-friendly demo experiences.

## Showcase scope

The platform includes:

- a Trustworthy AI landing page,
- a featured live demo,
- a demo catalog with search and filtering,
- a dedicated funding acknowledgement section.
